Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert text case?

Paste your text into the input area and click the desired case button (Uppercase, Lowercase, Title Case, etc.). The converted text appears instantly and can be copied.

What is Title Case?

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of each major word, following standard title capitalization rules. Perfect for headings and titles.

What's the difference between Camel Case and Snake Case?

Camel Case removes spaces and capitalizes each word (camelCaseExample). Snake Case replaces spaces with underscores (snake_case_example). Both are common in programming.

Can I convert large amounts of text?

Yes! There's no limit on text length. The tool handles everything from single words to entire documents instantly.
