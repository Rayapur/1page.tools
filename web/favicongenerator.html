Favicon Information

What is a Favicon?

A favicon is a small icon displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and address bars to represent your website.

Standard Sizes

16x16 (classic), 32x32 (standard), 48x48 (high-res), 64x64 (retina displays).

Best Practices

Use simple designs, high contrast, and test on different backgrounds and sizes.

File Formats

PNG (modern), ICO (legacy), SVG (vector). Most browsers support PNG favicons.
